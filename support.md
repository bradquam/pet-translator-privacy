## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I use the app?
1. Take a photo or record a short video of your pet (or choose one from your library)
2. Pick a personality that fits your pet's vibe
3. The app generates a funny caption — shuffle for more or edit it yourself
4. Tap "Hear It Speak" to hear the caption read aloud
5. Export and share with friends!

### What are HD Voices?
HD Voices use OpenAI's text-to-speech API to generate expressive, lifelike voice audio for your pet's caption. This feature requires an OpenAI API key, which you can add in Settings. Free on-device voices are always available as a fallback.

### How do I get an OpenAI API key?
1. Visit [platform.openai.com](https://platform.openai.com/)
2. Create an account or sign in
3. Navigate to API Keys and create a new key
4. Copy the key and paste it into Settings within the app

### Does the app upload my photos or videos?
No. Your photos and videos never leave your device. The only data sent externally is the caption text (for HD voice generation), which contains no personal information.

### Why doesn't the voice audio play in my shared photo?
Voice audio is embedded in video exports only. Photos are exported as static images with the caption overlay. To include voice audio, record a video instead.

### The app isn't working properly. What should I do?
- Make sure you have the latest version of the app installed
- Restart the app
- Check that you've granted camera and photo library permissions in your device settings
- If using HD Voices, verify your OpenAI API key is valid in the app's Settings
